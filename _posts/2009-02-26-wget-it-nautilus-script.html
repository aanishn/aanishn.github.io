Below is a 5min nautilus script which I wrote to get a feeling of the feature. It wgets a URI into the destination of your choice. Keep the script in ~/.gnome2/nautilus-scripts/wget-it. In nautilus navigate to the destination, right click->scipts->wget-it; paste the URI. done !!

---
#/bin/sh

URI=`zenity --entry --text="Enter the uri" --entry-text=http://`
filename=`basename $URI`
destdirname="${NAUTILUS_SCRIPT_CURRENT_URI##file://}"

wget "$URI"
mv "$filename" "$destdirname"

notify-send "$filename downloaded to $destdirname"

exit 0
